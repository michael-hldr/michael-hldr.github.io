Calculus

Avoid using the textbook problems when studying for exams. The OpenStax textbook mainly has procedural problems, similar to WeBWorK problems. While they are good at getting you to understand how to do the math yourself, they will not help you solve any word problems, which are much more important in engineering than simply doing the math.

This is the advice we were given in Winter 2024.
