Projects

This page lists anything I feel is worth publishing on this site, including course work. The list will expand as I do more projects.

I am currently working on a traffic study focusing on John Street between Leslie Street and Woodbine Avenue, which will attempt to find a solution to traffic problems along this section of John.

From April to August 2025, I worked on the West Aurora Network Study (WANS), a reimagining of the bus network in Aurora. You can find the final report here.

In early 2025, I looked into YRT Route 7, specifically to determine whether truncating the route at Humber College would be a good idea. YRT considered this in 2024 (archived), but did not implement the change in January 2025 as originally proposed. I completed the final report on April 7, 2025 and it can be found here. I will conduct a follow-up study in September or January to determine the effects of this change and whether more changes are needed.

Course work

In October 2024, I was required to write a design report for the CIV282 (Engineering Communication) course. I chose to write mine on potential efficiency improvements to Major Mackenzie West Terminal, such as more direct exits and traffic signal timing changes. While I am not confident in the costs I found while researching and the drawings could have been better (I hadn't learned AutoCAD yet), I think it is a good overview of ways to potentially improve the existing terminal. You can see the report here.

In January - April 2024, as part of the APS112 (Engineering Strategies & Practice 2) course, my team and I made a design for our client, Professor Jason Bazylak, specifically for his kids' lego play area. Please contact me if you would like the report.
