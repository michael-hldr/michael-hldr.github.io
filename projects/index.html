Projects

This page lists anything I feel is worth publishing on this site, including course work. The list may expand as I do more projects.

Starting January 2025, I am looking into YRT Route 7, specifically to determine whether truncating the route at Humber College would be a good idea. YRT considered this in 2024 (archived), but ultimately did not implement the change in January 2025. While YRT noted 90 trips are impacted on a typical weekday, how does this compare to the passengers who would benefit from the increased service on the rest of the route once it is truncated? I will post updates as I get closer to a conclusion, which may not necessarily be just truncating the route or leaving it as is.

Course work

In October 2024, I was required to write a design report for the CIV282 (Engineering Communication) course. I chose to write mine on potential efficiency improvements to Major Mackenzie West Terminal, such as more direct exits and traffic signal timing changes. While I am not confident in the costs I found while researching and the drawings could have been better, I think it is a good overview of ways to potentially improve the existing terminal. You can see the report here.
