Course work

In October 2024, I was required to write a design report for the CIV282 (Engineering Communication) course. I chose to write mine on potential efficiency improvements to Major Mackenzie West Terminal, such as more direct exits and traffic signal timing changes. While I am not confident in the costs I found while researching, I think it overall is a good overview of ways to potentially improve the existing terminal. You can see the report here.
